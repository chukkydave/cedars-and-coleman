About BUILD

Every human being has the innate potential to lead their lives and achieve their desired outcomes. Like Myles Munroe of blessed memory had rightly asserted; "The pessimist complains about the wind; the optimist expects it to change; the [leader] adjusts the sail." To lead your life as you wish to, you must build and put systematic structures in place, legacies that should eventually outlive you. The major problem of many societies especially in the 3rd world nations is their inability to provide a modest life for the people; therefore, anyone who must make progress must create his path and walk in it. The ones who will take up the bricks of their innate potentials as raw materials and with persistence, build for themselves such empires as they desire, will live like kings of their lives. Build is a manual, a guidebook that presents a series of proven principles that make for a life of lasting legacy.
