We Support Your Business Growth Plans

Cedars and Coleman is a business development company focused on supporting companies and institutions in driving their growth agenda through leadership development, cross-border trade facilitation, business structuring, advisory services and financing arrangements.

Leveraging our unique expertise and experience across a broad range of sectors and industries, we have designed our company to provide our clients across Africa with innovative and sustainable solutions to help them grow rapidly irrespective of their size or stage of development.

Our Purpose

Unlocking the growth potentials of African businesses through multi-layer support structures to create an economically viable continent.

We are the growth company.

About Benjamin Dike

Benjamin is a certified management consultant, leadership development practitioner, entrepreneur and professional banker. In the last 18 years, he has worked with small businesses and corporates, assisting them with enterprise development, financing options and structures and business process reengineering both in his professional work as a senior leader in the banking sector and a business coach to several companies. His over 25 years banking career spanned multiple functions and senior leadership roles.

Beyond his prior role as a business head in Standard Chartered Bank Nigeria, he has remained an industry leader in supporting the growth of Nigerian businesses through multiple initiatives and platforms, building cross-sectoral collaborations that provide relevant knowledge, financing solutions and advisory services. He has partnered with several key organizations to provide training and growth platforms to SMEs, including the International Finance Corporation, WeConnect International, Nigeria America Chamber of Commerce, Nigeria British Chamber of Commerce, Institute of Export Operations and Management, Manufacturers Association of Nigeria, etc.

Benjamin holds a doctorate degree in Credit Management; and an MBA from the University of Leicester in the United Kingdom. He has the professional membership of the Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria and is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Finance and Control of Nigeria, the Institute of Credit Administration and the Institute of Management Consultants.

About Daniel Chinagozi

Daniel Chinagozi is the Founder and CEO of Innovation Growth Hub, a Startup, Business, Technology and Creative Incubation Hub that provides mentorship, support services, community and co-work spaces to early growth startups with headquarters in Aba, and offices in Onitsha, Lagos, Umuahia Nigeria.

Having acquired a lot of training on Business and Enterprise Development locally and internationally, Daniel is a force to reckon with in the development of entrepreneurs and enterprises locally and internationally. He has personally trained over 4260 intending entrepreneurs and has raised 273 entrepreneurs running their own businesses and has personally helped open another 106 businesses in various industries and seats on the board of a number of SMEs and Afrilab- Association of Hubs across Africa.

Daniel is a trainer with an undying passion for entrepreneurship and organizational development. He is a Google Digital Skills Trainer for Haptics and Kinetics. He is also a Facebook Partner on the Boost Your Business with Facebook which together with his team has trained over 35,000 SMEs and counting.

A Business Development and Renewable Energy expert, TechOnRadio Presenter and Entrepreneur, Daniel is committed to seeing a World-Class Africa.
